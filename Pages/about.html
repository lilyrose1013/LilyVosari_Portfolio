About

I am a current senior at Parsons School of Design, majoring in Design and Technology following the creative technology pathway, and am based in New York City. I grew up right outside of the city in Rowayton, Connecticut, to an electrical engineer father and a designer mother. Growing up, this is where I developed my passion for creative technology, specifically website development and design, along with product design within the field. I grew up helping my father work on vintage cars, which developed my interest in technical design along with innovation and problem-solving skills. In addition, I grew up figure skating and still skate today. While training as a competitive figure skater, I learned the importance of discipline, attention to detail, and resilience.

I have interests in a variety of creative technology fields, including web development, product design, and creative coding, along with the design aspect of technical and speculative design. I find corporate web design and branding interesting, specifically the aspects of creating both a visually appealing and user-based design experience. I also have interests in product design, which creates financially viable products through the user-based design process.
