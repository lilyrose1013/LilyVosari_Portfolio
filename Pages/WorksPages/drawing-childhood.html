Physical and Immersive Art, April 2025

This work is a collective group project, where my teammates and I examined how to create an immersive environment that communicates the joys of childhood. We wanted to focus on standouts from our childhood like the Etch-a-Sketch, our bedrooms, the audio, along with size manipulation — with everything feeling and looking bigger than expected. In addition, we created a physical remote to resemble our hobbies of playing video games, with a joystick controller.
